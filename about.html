About Us

Fabtech Technical Campus,College of Engineering And Research Sangola

The Fabtech Technical Campus College of Engineering, was started by the Fabtech Education Society, Sangola. The Society itself was founded by Late Hon. Biraso Rupnar, an eminent Educationalist. The Institute made an humble beginning in 2011 Affiliated with ACITE today it is a full-fledged Engineering with a multifarious activities under its umbrella. The Institute has completed 12 years of its yeomen services for the cause of Technical Education.
